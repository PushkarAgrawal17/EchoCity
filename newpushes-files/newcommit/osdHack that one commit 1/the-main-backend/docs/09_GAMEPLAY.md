# EchoCity Gameplay

Version: 3.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the gameplay philosophy of EchoCity.

EchoCity is not primarily a detective game.

It is a living city simulation where an investigation emerges naturally
from the interactions of autonomous citizens.

The player's goal is not to "solve a puzzle."

The goal is to understand, observe, and influence a living social system.

---

# Core Vision

The city exists independently of the player.

Citizens continue living whether the player acts or not.

They

- observe
- remember
- gossip
- misunderstand
- spread rumors
- influence one another

The crime is only one event inside this living world.

---

# Player Role

The player is the Higher Self.

The player has no physical presence.

The player cannot

- move
- fight
- own items
- force actions

Instead the player subtly influences cognition.

The player watches the city evolve rather than directing it.

---

# The Living City

Every citizen owns

- identity
- location
- memories
- goals
- state

Knowledge is decentralized.

No citizen knows everything.

Truth spreads gradually.

Rumors mutate through conversation.

The city should appear alive even if the player does nothing.

---

# Crime

One deterministic crime exists.

Crime is the catalyst.

It gives citizens something to react to.

The investigation exists because the city reacted to the crime—not because the game spawned a quest.

---

# Knowledge Flow

Information always moves through the same pipeline.

Crime

↓

Witnesses

↓

Memories

↓

Conversations

↓

Rumors

↓

Player Observation

↓

Evidence

↓

Court

The player never receives privileged information.

Everything must be discovered through the city's social network.

---

# Simulation Loop

The simulation continues independently.

Each tick

1. Advance time

2. Execute schedules

3. Update agents

4. Run conversations

5. Spread gossip

6. Publish events

No player action pauses this loop.

---

# World Before The Player

When the player enters the simulation

the city has already been living.

The crime has already occurred.

Witnesses already remember.

Rumors have already begun spreading.

The player joins an existing world rather than starting one.

---

# Investigation

The player investigates by observing the city.

Typical flow

Observe

↓

Question citizens

↓

Compare conflicting stories

↓

Collect evidence

↓

Build a case

↓

Accuse a suspect

↓

Receive a verdict

Different investigation paths should naturally emerge depending on how information spreads through the city.

---

# EchoShell

EchoShell is the player's window into the city.

It exposes

Navigation

Investigation

Higher Self abilities

Court

EchoShell never contains gameplay logic.

It simply exposes systems already running inside the simulation.

---

# Higher Self

The player influences minds rather than bodies.

Possible influences include

- Suggest
- Remember
- Encourage
- Comfort
- Warn
- Connect
- Coincidence

These abilities never directly control citizens.

Instead they alter the information and circumstances that citizens reason about.

Citizens remain autonomous.

---

# Design Philosophy

The simulation owns reality.

The player influences reality.

AI enhances expression.

Deterministic systems decide truth.

Every gameplay mechanic should make the city feel more alive.

Whenever there is a choice between

adding another command

or

making citizens behave more believably,

choose the latter.

The city itself is the primary character of EchoCity.
